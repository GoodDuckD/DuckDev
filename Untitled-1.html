Roles

Here it will be said what is needed to get the roles

Youtuber Role

Requirements

  • 3 or more videos about F2Games
  • 150-200 subscribers

If the user qualifies for the role, you can send them this text (optional)

Congratulations, you qualify for the Youtuber role, you can publish your content in #promotion and notify members with Promotion Ping role. Remember that you can only mention the role every 24 hours.

Active Role

Requirements

  • Level 15
  • Less than two warns

If the user qualifies for the role, you can send them this text (optional)

Congratulations, you have the requirements to obtain the Active role. You can send images on the channels, remember to read #Rules so as not to send anything out of place

Artist Role

Requirements

  • Three arts related to F2Games

If the user qualifies for the role, you can send them this text (optional)

Congratulations, your art is good enough for the role. You now have access to #fan-art

Story Teller Role

Requirements

  • A good story about Gorebox

If the user qualifies for the role, you can send them this text (optional)

Congratulations, your story is good. You now have access to #gb-stories
